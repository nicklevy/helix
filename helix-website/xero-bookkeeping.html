Why Xero?

Xero is a globally recognized, cloud-based accounting platform designed to make financial admin simple, fast, and accessible everywhere. At Helix Business Services, we are proud to be Xero Certified Advisors. We believe it is the very best platform for modern businesses.

Real-time Collaboration

You, your team, and your accountant can all securely access your data at the same time, from any device with an internet connection.

Automated Bank Feeds

Transactions flow directly from your bank into Xero, making reconciliation lightning fast and drastically reducing manual data entry errors.

MTD Compliant

Xero is fully recognized by HMRC for Making Tax Digital, ensuring your VAT returns are submitted flawlessly directly from your ledger.

Massive App Ecosystem

Connect your CRM, point-of-sale system, inventory manager, or HR software seamlessly via Xero's massive third-party app marketplace.
