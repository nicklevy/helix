About Us

Driven by a belief that professional financial and HR backing is crucial to business success.

Partnering for Success

At Helix Business Services, we pride ourselves on delivering bespoke bookkeeping, accounting, and HR solutions tailored precisely to the unique needs of our clients. Based in Radlett, Hertfordshire, our dedicated team serves a wide variety of clients spanning sole traders, limited companies, and ambitious startups.

The Helix Story

Helix Business Services was founded with a singular, powerful mission: to help businesses grow by letting business owners focus on their actual business.

We saw too many passionate entrepreneurs sacrificing their weekends to manage VAT returns and HR compliance. By providing a cost-effective, professional, and reliable service, we take the administrative burden off your shoulders, giving you back the time and clarity needed to scale your operations successfully.

Why Choose Us?

Personalised Approach

We understand that every business is different. Our services flex to fit your exact requirements, whether that means weekly, monthly, or quarterly support.

Xero Specialists

Certified Practitioners

All-in-One Solution
