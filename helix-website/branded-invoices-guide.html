Setting up Branded Invoices

Make your business look incredibly professional by customizing your Xero invoice templates with your logo and brand colors.

Your invoice is often the last point of contact you have with a client during a transaction. A professional, branded invoice not only builds trust but also ensures all necessary legal information is clearly presented.

Step 1: Locate Invoice Settings

Click on your organization name in the top-left corner and select Settings. From there, find the Invoice Settings tile. This is where you manage all your "Branding Themes".

Step 2: Upload Your Logo

For each branding theme, you can upload a high-resolution logo (JPG, PNG, or GIF). We recommend a high-quality file with a transparent background for the cleanest look. Click the Upload Logo button on your chosen theme to begin.

Step 3: Customizing Layout & Colors

Click the Options button next to your theme and select Edit. Here you can change:

  • Margins: Adjust the spacing to fit your letterhead.
  • Font Style & Size: Match your company's typeface (limited to web-safe options).
  • Headings: Choose which columns and information to display.

Step 4: Adding Payment Terms

At the bottom of the Edit screen, you'll find the "Payment Services" and "Payment Advice" sections. Clearly state your bank details and your standard payment terms (e.g., "Due within 14 days").

Pro Tip

Including a "Pay Now" link via Stripe or GoCardless can reduce payment wait times by up to 50%!

Step 5: Preview Your Design

Before sending to a client, always click Options > Preview. Check both the PDF version and the Online version to ensure your logo is clearly visible and all text is legible.
