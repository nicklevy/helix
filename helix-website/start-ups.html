Bookkeeping for Start Ups

Establishing the right financial foundation from day one, preparing your new venture for sustainable growth.

Start Strong, Scale Fast

When launching a new business, the financial setup can be overwhelming. Making the right structural decisions and implementing robust accounting systems early on is critical to avoiding costly mistakes down the line. We specialise in bringing order and strategy to start up finances.

Entity Setup & Registration

We guide you through the process of determining the best legal structure (Sole Trader vs Limited Company) and handle all necessary registrations with HMRC and Companies House.

Cloud Accounting Implementation

We set you up on Xero perfectly from day one. You'll have an intuitive dashboard showing your real-time cash position, integrated directly with your business bank accounts.

Cash Flow Forecasting

Start ups live and die by cash flow. We help you project your runway, understand your burn rate, and prepare financial models vital for securing investment or loans.

Future-Proofing

As your team grows, you'll need robust HR support. As your revenue grows, you'll need VAT registration. We build the processes today that will handle your operations tomorrow.
